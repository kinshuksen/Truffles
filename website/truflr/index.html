Truflr //

go places, see stuff, find truffles

Find Out More

truflr is an engagement focused point of interest exploration platform powered by Open Government Data

Tourists and locals alike are incentivized to sniff out hidden truffles around them. Its a pick up and play app that takes away the need for planning adventures. It encourages users across multiple generations to engage with public places, spaces and attractions.

Endless challenges await players resulting in out of the way places being sniffed out. Players add to their bag of truffles as they go to keep their piggy fed and happy

iBeacon Powered Truffle Hunting

Let's go hunting! - iBeacons (Golden Truffles) spread out across an area present a fun challenge to find them all using the app's Thermometer functionality. This is using iBeacon technology to gauge proximity. Users are rewarded with a special Golden Truffle for their efforts

Thrill of the Find

Get your hunt on using Bluetooth to find Golden Truffles

Engaged Tourists

This app is made possible by the awesome data exposed by SA Tourism Commission and the Adelaide City Council. Its reach can be broadened to incorporate other data sets across Australia and the world as they are exposed.

Datasets exposed from http://data.sa.gov.au such as https://data.sa.gov.au/data/dataset/australian-tourism-data-warehouse-api, https://data.sa.gov.au/data/dataset/monuments, https://data.sa.gov.au/data/dataset/trails-great-buildings-sights-public-art-api were key in this proof of concept stage.
